# Supported Websites

This extension is designed to work as a general-purpose tool, but we are maintaining a list of news websites that are confirmed to work well with the bypass features.

**Help us grow this list!**
If you find a news site that works great with this extension, please submit it to us through telegram(available on our website and the extension)!
